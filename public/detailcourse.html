Course Description

This comprehensive Algebra course is designed to equip students with a solid understanding of algebraic concepts, from basic arithmetic to complex equations. You will learn to manipulate expressions, solve linear and quadratic equations, and explore functions and graphs. Real-world applications in science, finance, and technology are embedded throughout the course to deepen understanding and relevance.

The course includes interactive problem sets, video tutorials, and AI-powered feedback through DiaLogos. You'll also engage in collaborative activities and projects that strengthen your mathematical thinking and analytical skills.

By the end of this course, you will be confident in using algebraic methods to approach real-world problems and be well-prepared for advanced studies in mathematics, engineering, and related fields.

What You'll Learn

  • What is Algebra?
  • Variables and Constants
  • Basic Algebraic Operations
  • Linear Equations
  • Quadratic Equations
  • Systems of Equations
  • Algebra in Science
  • Algebra in Finance
  • Problem Solving Strategies

Course Specifications

Subject Area: Mathematics – Algebra

This course is part of the Mathematics curriculum, focused on Foundations of Algebra. You'll learn to solve equations, work with expressions, and understand applications in science and finance.

Course Format: Private, Semi-Private, Group

  • Private – One-on-one learning tailored to your pace and needs.
  • Semi-Private – Small group (2–3 students), more personalized interaction.
  • Group – Up to 8 students, collaborative and structured learning.

Schedule & Duration

Start Date: September 15
Duration: 8 weeks
Sessions: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 5:00–6:30 PM
Optional weekend sessions available for review and Q&A.

Contest Preparation: Math Kangaroo

Aligned with the Canadian Math Kangaroo Contest, this course enhances logical reasoning and creative problem-solving—key skills for contest success. Includes practice sets modeled on real contest problems.

Level: Beginner

Designed for beginners with no prior algebra experience. Concepts are taught step-by-step, ensuring confidence and clarity at every stage.
